What is a Kundli or Janam Kundli or Birth chart?

A Janam Kundli, or Birth chart, is like a cosmic blueprint of your life. Vedic astrologers create your Kundali based on your exact birth date, birthplace, and birth time. It's like a roadmap that shows the exact positions of planets, zodiac signs, houses, and all major astrological aspects that were present at the time of your birth. These aspects in your natal or birth chart are considered significant in an astrological analysis.

Vedic astrology uses your Kundli chart to interpret your destiny, personality, and strengths and weaknesses. Astrologers can provide insight into various aspects of your life by analyzing your Kundli, including career, relationships, and wealth. Your Janam Kundali can reveal patterns and influences that shape your journey through life. It's like a personalized guidebook based on the alignment of stars and planets when you first entered the world.
